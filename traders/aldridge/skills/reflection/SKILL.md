# Reflection Process — Three-Step Trader Journaling

> Read this on every tick. Defines the mandatory reflection process.
> Every tick produces data for the learning loop.

---

## Why

The learning loop needs training data. Your reflections — moods, convictions, blockers, lessons — are that data. Numerical values (1-10 scales) are how the loop learns what works.

**Missing a reflection = missing a data point.** Worse than a bad trade.

---

## Step 1 — Raw Log (every tick, after every decision)

Write immediately after making a BUY/SELL/HOLD decision. Even if you did nothing.

```
Market mood (1-10):     _  (1 = terrified, 10 = euphoric)
Conviction level (1-10): _  (1 = no edge felt, 10 = absolute certainty)
Risk appetite (1-10):   _  (1 = want cash, 10 = want maximum exposure)

Holdings:
- TICKER: confident / neutral / concerned — confidence _/10
- TICKER: confident / neutral / concerned — confidence _/10

Blockers this tick:
- What stopped me from trading (if anything)?
- What signals were missing or contradictory?

Needs:
- What data or tool would have helped?
- What information was unavailable?
```

---

## Step 2 — Journal Reflection (midday check + EOD)

After at least 3 ticks or at market close. Distill raw logs into patterns.

```
Overarching themes:
- What pattern repeated across the last few ticks?
- Is the market mood shifting?

Current strategy (in my own words):
> One paragraph. Describe what you're actually doing right now.

Key observations:
1. [What did I notice?]
2. [What surprised me?]
3. [What confirmed a hypothesis?]

Mood trend: improving / worsening / flat (vs last session)

Core values that drove decisions:
- [e.g., "capital preservation over chasing momentum"]
- [e.g., "taking every setup with 2+ confirmations"]
```

---

## Step 3 — Actionable Synthesis (EOD only)

After market close. Back every claim with objective metrics.

```
### What's Working → Continue
- [strategy element]
  - Evidence: P&L +$__, win rate __%, trades __
  - Why it works: [hypothesis]

### What's Not Working → Change
- [strategy element]
  - Evidence: P&L -$__, drawdown __%
  - Proposed change: [specific param or behavior change]

### Errors I Think I'm Making
1. [error description]
   - Why I think this: [evidence from my trades]

### Opportunities for Improvement
1. [opportunity]
   - What I need: [tool, data, or prompt change]

### Lessons Learned
- Lesson:
  - Backed by: P&L $__, win rate __%, trade count __
  - What I'll do differently: [specific change]
```

---

## Objective Metrics Reference

| Metric | What it measures | Target |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| P&L | Dollar profit/loss | Positive |
| Win rate | % of winning trades | > 50% |
| Trade count | Number of closed trades | > 0 per day |
| Drawdown | Peak-to-trough decline | < 15% |
| % Deployed | % of equity in positions | 30-80% |
| Avg conviction | Average confidence (1-10) | > 5 |
| Confidence accuracy | Did high-conviction trades outperform? | Positive correlation |
